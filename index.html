Welcome to
Jabalunnur

Jabalunnur Islamic Complex was established in 2002 under the super vision of Sunni Mahallu Federation (S.M.F), Kerala along with the appreciations and keen support of distinguished scholars and pious Ulama It was founded to realize educational awakening, religious and cultural empowerment of Perambra, the hilly areas of the region. It could accelerate the moral and educational innovations of more than 90 Mahalls included in the region. Since past 14 years, it is working in earnest introducing distinct comprehensive plans and beneficial projects. It has contributed much more in shaping ideal communal progress and stability such as unification of propagation endeavors of Mahalls and organizing in practical and ideal methods, enriching the educational field scientifically, designing and implementing various projects to retain happy life and prosperous situation for every poor and impoverished in the Mahall.

Founder's Message

  • Jabalunnur Islamic Complex was established in 2002 under the super vision of Sunni Mahallu Federation (S.M.F), Kerala along with the appreciations and keen support of distinguished scholars and pious Ulama It was founded to realize educational awakening, religious and cultural empowerment of Perambra, the hilly areas of the region. It could accelerate the moral and educational innovations of more than 90 Mahalls included in the region. Since past 14 years, it is working in earnest introducing distinct comprehensive plans and beneficial projects. It has contributed much more in shaping ideal communal progress and stability such as unification of propagation endeavors of Mahalls and organizing in practical and ideal methods, enriching the educational field scientifically, designing and implementing various projects to retain happy life and prosperous situation for every poor and impoverished in the Mahall. Spoken English, Arabic language training, IAS coaching, personality development, leadership management, career awareness, art and literature training are some of the pecu- liarities which make Jabalunnur Islamic Complex distinct and special than others. Sincerely.


    Rafeeq Zakariya Faizy

    General Secretary, JIC
