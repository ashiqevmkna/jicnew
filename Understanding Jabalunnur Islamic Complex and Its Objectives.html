Understanding Jabalunnur Islamic
Complex and Its Objectives.

  • Natasha Pawel
  • Dec 28, 2022
  • 716 views

Jabalunnur Islamic Complex was established in 2002 in Perambra, a hilly region in Kerala, under the supervision of Sunni Mahallu Federation (S.M.F), Kerala. The institution was founded with the aim of promoting educational awakening, religious and cultural empowerment, and community development. Over the past 14 years, Jabalunnur Islamic Complex has introduced several comprehensive plans and projects that have contributed to the progress and stability of the region.

The primary objective of Jabalunnur Islamic Complex is to promote communal harmony and uplift the underprivileged sections of society. The institution has been able to achieve this objective through various initiatives such as providing financial assistance to impoverished families, organizing health and sanitation programs, setting up libraries and reading rooms, and promoting the unification of propagation endeavors of Mahalls.

Jabalunnur Islamic Complex also plays a significant role in the education sector of the region. The institution offers a range of courses in various disciplines, including Islamic studies, Arabic language, and computer education. It provides vocational training programs to equip students with skills that will help them find suitable employment opportunities. Through its educational initiatives, Jabalunnur Islamic Complex has been able to empower students and enable them to become responsible citizens.


The institution has also contributed significantly to religious empowerment in the region. It offers courses in Islamic studies and Arabic language to enable students to understand the teachings of Islam better. It conducts seminars and workshops to promote religious harmony and tolerance. Jabalunnur Islamic Complex has been able to create a sense of religious awakening among the people of Perambra and the surrounding regions, resulting in increased religious awareness and a more significant commitment to the principles of Islam.

In conclusion, Jabalunnur Islamic Complex has been instrumental in promoting communal harmony, uplifting the underprivileged sections of society, enriching the educational field, and promoting religious empowerment in Perambra and its surrounding regions. The institution has made significant contributions to the progress and stability of the region over the past 14 years, and its efforts have been widely acknowledged.

One of the notable projects introduced by Jabalunnur Islamic Complex is the construction of a masjid, which is an Islamic prayer hall. The masjid, which is situated on a hilltop, is a significant landmark in the region and serves as a place of worship for the local Muslim community. It also provides a venue for Islamic seminars and other religious events, which promote religious awareness and understanding among the people.

Moreover, Jabalunnur Islamic Complex has been able to establish itself as a center of community development by actively engaging in various social and economic initiatives. The institution has initiated microfinance schemes and livelihood support programs, which have empowered women and other marginalized groups in the community. It has also set up a community center, which provides a platform for community members to come together, share their experiences, and find solutions to their problems. Through its efforts, Jabalunnur Islamic Complex has been able to create a vibrant and cohesive community, which is committed to the development and progress of the region.
